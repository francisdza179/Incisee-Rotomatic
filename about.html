Our Story

Incise Rotomatic LLP

Founded as part of the Incise Group, Incise Rotomatic LLP has grown to become a trusted name in precision die-cutting and web converting — serving India's most demanding OEM and industrial customers from our facility in Chennai.

Our IATF 16949:2016 certification is not just a badge — it is the foundation of every process, every component, and every relationship we build. With in-house rotary die-cutting, VMC machining, and CNC capabilities, we deliver complete precision manufacturing under one roof.

Our Journey

Milestones That Define Us

2015
The Incise Group is Founded

The Incise Group begins its journey in precision manufacturing, establishing core capabilities in die-cutting and specialty component fabrication in Chennai, Tamil Nadu.

2017
Incise Rotomatic LLP Established

Incise Rotomatic is incorporated as a dedicated web converting and rotary die-cutting arm of the group, focusing on high-precision component manufacturing for automotive and electronics sectors.

2019
First OEM Supply Contract

Secured first Tier-1 automotive supply agreement with Ashok Leyland for precision VHB tape die-cuts — a significant milestone validating our manufacturing quality.

2021
IATF 16949:2016 Certification

Achieved IATF 16949:2016 certification — the global automotive quality management standard — formalising our commitment to zero-defect manufacturing and opening new OEM opportunities.

2022
Maruti Suzuki Partnership

Became an approved supplier for Maruti Suzuki's Airbag Fix Tape programme, cementing our position as a precision automotive component manufacturer.

Today
₹140M+ Turnover, 4 Industries

Serving automotive OEMs, electronics leaders, medical device manufacturers and industrial clients across India — with a portfolio of 12+ precision product lines and growing.
