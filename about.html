Who We Are

Precision at the Core

A decade of engineering excellence, IATF-certified quality, and deep material expertise — shaping the die-cutting landscape in India.

Our Story

Incisee Rotomatic Pvt. Ltd.

Incisee Rotomatic, a member of the Incise Group established in 2018, is a premier provider of precision die-cut and web converting solutions. We specialise in high-performance products including Pressure Relief Valves, ePTFE Membranes, and Thermal Pads for the automotive, EV, and electronics industries.

Our operations are IATF 16949 certified, ensuring world-class quality systems and full material traceability for global OEMs. We prioritize dimensional accuracy and on-time delivery to provide reliable, long-life components for the most demanding applications. By combining technical expertise with manufacturing excellence, we enable our partners to meet the highest industry standards.

Our Journey

Milestones That Define Us

2015
Incise Precision Founded

Established as an Engineering Solutions Consultancy to kickstart precision engineering and design capabilities.

2016
Machining Commencement

Commenced manufacturing operations with high-precision machining, serving the automotive and valve industries.

2017
EV Battery Enclosures

Expanded manufacturing capabilities into fabricating EV Battery Enclosures for electric vehicle manufacturers.

2018
Incise Rotomatic Launched

Launched Incise Rotomatic to deliver advanced web converting and high-precision rotary die-cutting solutions.

2020
Pvt. Ltd. Restructuring & Rebranding

Restructured operations and rebranded the web converting division officially as Incisee Rotomatic Pvt. Ltd.

2021
Strategic Partnership

Forged a strategic partnership with Caltech Adh Solution, boosting materials engineering and advisory capabilities.

2022
₹82M Revenue & Visli Launch

Reached ₹82 Million in revenue and launched Visli as our brand for premium corporate gifting and cookware.

2023
Revenue Growth to ₹114M

Achieved key sales milestones, climbing to ₹114 Million in revenue with expanded customer programs.

2024
Visli Impex Added

Officially added Visli Impex to the Group, strengthening our retail operations and domestic reach.

2025
New Company-Owned Facility

Moved into a new company-owned 1,200 sq. m plant in Chennai, achieving 20% year-over-year growth.
