Our Story

Incisee Rotomatic LLP

Founded as part of the Incisee Group, Incisee Rotomatic LLP has grown to become a trusted name in precision die-cutting and web converting — serving India's most demanding OEM and industrial customers from our facility in Chennai.

Our IATF 16949:2016 certification is not just a badge — it is the foundation of every process, every component, and every relationship we build. With in-house rotary die-cutting, VMC machining, and CNC capabilities, we deliver complete precision manufacturing under one roof.

Our Journey

Milestones That Define Us

2015
Incise Precision Founded

Established as an Engineering Solutions Consultancy to kickstart precision engineering and design capabilities.

2016
VMC Machining Commencement

Commenced manufacturing operations with high-precision VMC machining, serving the automotive and valve industries.

2017
EV Battery Enclosures

Expanded manufacturing capabilities into fabricating EV Battery Enclosures for electric vehicle manufacturers.

2018
Incise Rotomatic Launched

Launched Incise Rotomatic to deliver advanced web converting and high-precision rotary die-cutting solutions.

2020
LLP Restructuring & Rebranding

Restructured operations and rebranded the web converting division officially as Incisee Rotomatic LLP.

2021
Strategic Partnership

Forged a strategic partnership with Caltech Adh Solution, boosting materials engineering and advisory capabilities.

2022
₹82M Revenue & Visli Launch

Reached ₹82 Million in annual revenue and launched Visli as our brand for premium corporate gifting and cookware.

2023
Revenue Growth to ₹114M

Achieved key sales milestones, climbing to ₹114 Million in annual revenue with expanded customer programs.

2024
Visli Impex Added

Officially added Visli Impex to the Group, strengthening our retail operations and domestic reach.

2025
New Facility & ₹161M Revenue

Moved into a new company-owned 1,200 sq. m plant in Chennai, achieving ₹161 Million revenue at 20% YOY growth.
