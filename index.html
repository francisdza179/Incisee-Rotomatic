Web Converting Specialists

Incisee Rotomatic LLP is a Chennai-based, IATF 16949:2016 certified manufacturer within the Incisee Group. We specialise in custom rotary and flatbed die-cutting, web converting, and precision component fabrication — serving the most demanding industries in India and globally.
