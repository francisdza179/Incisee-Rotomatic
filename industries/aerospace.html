Heavy-Duty Solutions for Aerospace Applications

Aerospace environments are unforgiving — extreme temperatures, pressure differentials, chemical exposure, and mechanical stress. Our die-cut components are engineered with material expertise to perform reliably under these conditions.

Foam Gaskets

Open and closed-cell foam gaskets for IP-rated enclosure sealing, panel weatherproofing, and vibration isolation in industrial equipment.
Silicone Compression Pads

High-temperature rated silicone compression pads for industrial sealing, thermal management, and vibration dampening applications.
Aerospace ePTFE Vents

Chemical-resistant ePTFE pressure equalisation membranes for outdoor control panels, industrial enclosures, and harsh-environment equipment.
Sealing Gaskets

Custom die-cut sealing gaskets for industrial flanges, pipe connections, pump assemblies, and mechanical equipment covers.
