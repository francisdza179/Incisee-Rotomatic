Precision Parts for the Automotive Industry

Incisee Rotomatic is a certified Tier-1 and Tier-2 supplier to India's automotive sector. Our IATF 16949:2016 quality management system ensures that every die-cut component meets the zero-defect standard expected by OEM assembly lines.

We work with OEMs and Tier-1s on everything from sealing gaskets and protective film to complex tape die-cuts with precise adhesive placement — all manufactured to drawing and delivered on schedule.

Automotive Product Lines

VHB Tape Die-Cuts

High-strength structural bonding tape, precision die-cut to exact dimensions for Ashok Leyland assembly applications. OEM-approved specification.
Airbag Fix Tape

Approved airbag assembly tape die-cuts for Maruti Suzuki's production line. Critical safety component manufactured to exact OEM tolerances.
Sealing Gaskets

Precision-cut sealing gaskets for automotive door panels, engine bays, and electronic control unit (ECU) housings.
Protective Films

Die-cut protective film solutions for automotive paint protection, interior component protection during assembly, and surface masking.
ePTFE Sensor Vents

Pressure-equalising ePTFE membrane vents for automotive sensors, ECUs, and lighting assemblies — blocking water and dust while allowing airflow.
Foam Gaskets

Compression foam gaskets for NVH (noise, vibration, harshness) management, interior panel cushioning, and weather sealing in automotive applications.
