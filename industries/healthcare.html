Precision Components for Healthcare Devices

Healthcare device manufacturing demands the highest standards of dimensional accuracy, material purity, and quality documentation. Our IATF 16949:2016 quality framework translates directly to the rigorous requirements of medical component manufacturing.

From die-cut electrode pads that must maintain consistent electrical contact, to ePTFE venting membranes that must maintain sterile barriers — our precision cutting ensures every component performs to specification.

Heart Monitor Electrode Pads

Precision die-cut ECG/EEG electrode pads with consistent adhesive coverage and electrical contact area. Manufactured to tight dimensional tolerances.
ePTFE Sterile Vents

Hydrophobic ePTFE membranes for medical device venting applications — maintaining sterile barriers while allowing pressure equalisation and airflow.
Device Sealing Components

Custom die-cut gaskets and sealing membranes for medical device housings, IV set components, and surgical instrument assemblies.
Quality Alignment

Our IATF 16949:2016 quality management system — designed for zero-defect automotive manufacturing — provides a strong quality foundation for medical component production, including full traceability and process documentation.
