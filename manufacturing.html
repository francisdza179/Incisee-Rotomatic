Class 10,000 Clean Room Facility

Our manufacturing shop floor features an IATF-compliant, ISO Class 7 (Class 10,000) clean room environment designed specifically for healthcare assemblies, skin-contact adhesives, wearable patch components, and sensitive ePTFE electronics venting.

Housing two multi-station rotary die-cutting systems (including our IDENT UK and HOACO lines), this facility ensures particle-free, hygienic, and regulatory-compliant manufacturing for global OEMs.
