ePTFE Membranes

Expanded polytetrafluoroethylene (ePTFE) membranes for precision venting, filtration, and sterile barrier applications. Precision die-cut to exact dimensions with adhesive backing options for easy peel-and-apply integration.

Our ePTFE membranes combine extreme hydrophobicity with excellent breathability — blocking liquid water and dust while allowing air and water vapour to pass freely. Chemically inert, thermally stable, and available in multiple thickness and porosity grades.

Key Properties

💧
Hydrophobic

Repels liquid water while remaining breathable — ideal for weatherproofing seals without blocking pressure equalisation.

🌬️
Breathable

Microporous structure allows air and water vapour transmission — enabling pressure equalisation without liquid ingress.

🧪
Chemically Inert

PTFE is one of the most chemically resistant polymers available — stable in the presence of oils, fuels, solvents, and aggressive media.
